Hello again!

I’m Dinara! A junior UX Designer and Google career certificate holder :D

I’ve explored ice hockey, English learning/teaching, and software development before finally choosing UX design as my career path.

I am passionate about the idea of contributing to a more peaceful and connected, happier and caring world through the stories in my design work.

Every person matters. Every story matters. Keeping that in mind, I feel honored to be responsible for making more inclusive communities.

I enjoy different modes of storytelling: writing, songwriting, painting, drawing, and playing musical instruments.
